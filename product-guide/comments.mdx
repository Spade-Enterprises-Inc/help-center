---
title: Comments
---

The Comments tab enables efficient collaboration and note-taking among procurement team members.

![Dashboard Filters](/images/gifs_images/comments.png)

- **Enter Comments**: You can add new comments to document discussions, notes, or important updates related to the deal.
- **Edit Comments**: Existing comments can be edited to update or correct information as needed. You can only edit your own comments.
- **Delete Comments**: Comments can be removed if they are no longer relevant or were added by mistake. You can only remove your own comments.

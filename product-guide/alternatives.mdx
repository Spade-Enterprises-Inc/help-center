title: Alternatives
---

Leveraging our proprietary taxonomy, we map the supplier to all of its relevant categories and display alternative vendors. This feature helps you compare options and make informed decisions during evaluations and replacement workflows.

![Dashboard Filters](/images/gifs_images/dealmanagement-alternatives.png)

This tab is only populated when the supplier associated with the deal is in our system. If you would like Airflip to categorize a new supplier, please email support@airflip.com or send us a support message in the app.
